Music

This is a projected work, for I aim to create percussion recordings eventually. I also aim to extend my interdisciplinary pursuits in the direction of music, given the history, philosophy and mathematics by which music can be analyzed or interpreted.

I'm primarily focused on percussion and hope to provide audio recordings of my experiments with percussive combos, rhythms and time signatures. Miles Davis' original fusion of the late 60s and early 70s. Steve Reich's minimalist compositions, and Ravi Shankar's classical music are the greatest sources of my musical inspiration.

“Sometimes you have to play a long time to be able to play like yourself.”
— Miles Davis
